Key Features

User Management

Users can register, login, and manage their e-books. Features include requesting, reading, and returning e-books.

Librarian Dashboard

Librarians can manage sections, add new e-books, issue and revoke access, and monitor e-book statuses.

Advanced Search

Users can search for e-books or sections by various criteria such as author, title, or section.

Feedback System

Users can provide feedback on e-books, helping to improve the library's offerings.

Multi-Language Support

Our system supports multiple languages seamlessly with UTF-8 encoding.

Access Control

Librarians can easily control and monitor e-book access for different users, ensuring security and proper management.

Book Availability & Management

Check the availability of e-books and manage their status. Librarians can update the library with new entries and ensure proper categorization.

User Actions on Books

Users can perform various actions like viewing details, issuing, and returning e-books, providing a complete e-book management experience.

Category & Section Browsing

Browse through different sections and categories to find e-books of interest. Efficient organization makes it easy to navigate the library.

Dashboard Statistics

Get insights into library usage with detailed statistics on users, books, and transactions.

Activity Logs

Track and monitor activities within the library system, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Role Management

Manage roles for different users, allowing specific access and functionalities based on their roles within the system.
